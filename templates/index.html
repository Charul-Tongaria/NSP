COVID-19 Data Analysis

Explore the global impact of COVID-19 on Health, Education, and Vaccination efforts

About This Project

The COVID-19 pandemic has had a profound impact on the world, affecting virtually every aspect of daily life, including health, education, and economic systems. This project aims to provide a detailed analysis of the available COVID-19 data, focusing on key areas such as:

  • Health Impact: We analyze the spread of the virus, the number of cases, and the mortality rates across different countries.
  • Liver Cancer Research: The project investigates how the pandemic has influenced liver cancer treatments and outcomes, particularly in countries that were severely affected by COVID-19.
  • Educational Disruption: We study how COVID-19 impacted education globally, tracking school closures, shifts to online learning, and the long-term effects on students' academic progress.
  • Vaccination Efforts: The analysis extends to vaccination campaigns, comparing the rate of vaccinations in different countries and how effective these campaigns have been in curbing the spread of the virus.
